Our Story

How DeployZ came to be and what drives us forward

The Beginning

DeployZ was born out of a simple need - making web deployment easier for developers. We noticed that many developers struggled with complex deployment processes, especially when working on small projects or prototypes.

Our Vision

Our vision is to democratize web deployment by making it accessible to everyone, from seasoned developers to beginners just starting their journey. We believe in the power of simplicity.

Open Source

DeployZ is proudly open source. We believe in the power of community collaboration and transparency. Anyone can contribute, review, or modify the code to suit their needs.
